Escanor - The Lion's Sin of Pride

Escanor is one of the most powerful characters in the anime and manga series Nanatsu no Taizai (The Seven Deadly Sins). Known as the Lion's Sin of Pride, his strength increases during the day and reaches its peak at noon. Despite his prideful nature during the day, he is humble and gentle at night. Escanor's personality and incredible abilities make him a fan-favorite character.

Escanor possesses the unique power called Sunshine, which makes him stronger as the sun rises. He wields the mighty Divine Axe Rhitta and can defeat even the strongest enemies at the height of his power.

To learn more about Escanor, visit the Nanatsu no Taizai Wiki.

Escanor's Abilities

  • Sunshine: Power grows with sunlight, strongest at noon
  • Divine Axe Rhitta: His signature weapon
  • Immense physical strength and combat skill
  • Near invincibility during peak power

Other Facts

  1. Nickname: The Lion's Sin of Pride
  2. Personality changes drastically between day and night
  3. Known for his famous line: "Who decided I was the one to fear? I am the one to fear!"
